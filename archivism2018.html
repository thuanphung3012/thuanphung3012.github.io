ARCHIVISM 2018
Hidden Archive x Shinesium

Archivism 2018 is a collaboration between me with Hidden Archive and Shinesium. The event has been created a wave in the fashion industry of Vietnam, because of its unique intention.This event wanted to bring archival fashion's culture closer to the audiences, and it had successfully achieved its purpose. Archivism 2018 showcase over 40 archive fashion piece from various brands. For instance: Undercover, Issey Miyake, Yohji Yamamoto, Kapital, Raf Simons, Helmut Lang, etc.

This showcase was also a playground where everybody could gather to share their experiences in the industry (250 people were coming to the event). Achivism 2018 was an inspirational show to encourage the audiences to be creative and to treasure the history of fashion along with art. the event also caught the media about its unique and how inspiration it was.

Role : My major role was creating visual for the exhibition such as book, tickets and posters. . My minor role was proposing ideas and invited individual talented artist/model to the collaboration. The process was planned before 6 months and proceed from crafting ideas to build the reality show. It started with photoshoot then we focused on marketing the exhibition by publishing the campaign and video. This project was contributed and included 20 people, there were around nearly 300 participations in 2 days during the show.

Contributors : Hidden Archive, Shinesium, Le Minh, Thanh Tien Nguyen, Freddy Nguyen, Nguyen Hoang Tho, Randy Ng, Nhat Truong, Tuong Danh, Phat Trien, Dang Chi Khang, Thu Anh Ho, Quynh Nguyen, Allison Nguyen, VSSG
