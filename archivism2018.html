ARCHIVISM 2018 :
HIDDEN ARCHIVE x SHINESIUM

Archivism 2018 was a collaboration between us, Hidden Archive, and Shinesium. The event has created a wave in the fashion industry of Vietnam, because of its unique intention. This event wanted to bring archival fashion’s culture closer to Vietnamese audiences, and it had successfully achieved its purpose. Archisvism 2018 showcased over 40 archive fashion pieces from various brands. For instance:
- Undercover, Issey Miyake, Yohji Yamamoto, Kapital, etc.
- This showcase was also a playground where people could gather and share their experiences in the industry (over 250 people attended to the event)
- Archivism 2018 was an inspirational show to encourage the audiences to be creative and to treasure the history of fashion along with art. The event also caught the media’s attention about its unique and how inspiration it was.
