My name is Thuan Phung. I’m a graphic designer who lives in New York.
Art is the combination of science and aesthetic to bring to life human ideas. Through typography, branding and a focus on user experience, I’m looking forward to collaborating and assist to the client’s problem or in need of visual communication for brand identity. I believe in the philosophy of art and design which work on the concepts, expression, interpretation, and finally representation. Every step of design is closely related to each other and when we integrate it will generate a beautiful and functional product.
Driven by technology and futuristic concept, the mindset of being a graphic designer is more complicated than just a “designer”. The concept of 3D and virtual reality will raise and whom graphic designers will be required more skills to meet the expectation. For that reason, I will continue studying at The New School to achieve a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Technology and Design.
