PROJECT 001 - BJORK HOMOGENIC



I first saw the poster “Bjork” was framed and hung on the 7th floor in my sophomore year at Parsons. The term “Bjork” is so distinctive and an odd-sounding that I’ve never heard about. It took me a year to learn how to pronounce it by replacing the letter “J” with “Y”, so it says as /B-york/ and also Bjork is an artsy person that obsesses about the flourishing Utopia world. In particular, the album “Homogenic” specifically demonstrated that her visionary about this future world created robots in 1997. “All is full of love” is the surreal music video directed by Chris Cunningham which is a subgenre of techno/ambient music. Lately, the movie “Ex Machina” has inspired by Bjork and both videos are implying about the love or emotion between robots.
