PROJECT 002 - MTA TRANSIT



I’ve been to Standard Manual bookstore in Brooklyn, NY, and then got inspired by MTA Graphics Standards Manual Book. So I impulsively rebrand MTA System following the dystopian concept where full of technology and equipment are a part of everyone's daily life. Moreover, I envision how New York will be compared to Los Angeles from the scene of Blade Runner 2049. The theme is the so-called Cyberpunk.
