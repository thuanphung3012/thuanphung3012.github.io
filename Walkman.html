SONY SPORTS WALKMAN

After finishing my first two projects, I want to push myself more into creating a product without an existing online mockup. In doing this, I hope to pick up more 3D modeling skills along the way and re-use the product for commercial or personal purposes.
With project 003, I was lucky to have found that Blender is free software that is still in progress for user experience testings. I learned most of the basic concepts and workflow of Blender within 3 months. I started out building a general shape and then worked towards adding smaller details.
When I was finally comfortable with using Blender, I started researching 3D designers and the MR3000 device designed by Joshua Cotter is my favorite gadget in the list. So I made it my goal to build a device that appears similar to the MR3000, which turned out to be a Walkman.
